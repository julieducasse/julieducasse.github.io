In short

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Primorska in Slovenia. I am working at the HICUP LAB and I am also part of the InnoRenew project.

During my thesis, I worked on the accessibility of maps and diagrams for visually impaired people. I investigated to what extent the physicality and reconfigurability of tangible interfaces could improve the accessibility of maps and diagrams, through the design, development and evaluation of tabletop tangible interfaces.
